What is ClickCare?

ClickCare is a website that primarily offers online assistance to people who experience Musculoskeletal Disorders (MSD). Here's how it works:

  • 1. Enter your personal information (Name, Home Address, Age, etc.)
  • 2. Answer some questions regarding your discomfort.
  • 3. That's it! Based on your answers, we'll provide you with the possible causes of your symptoms.

When you have the chance to consult with a specialist, you can present these results for their reference, saving you time and effort!
